# **Scholarly Research Assistant**

## **Problem Statement**
    Researchers around the world struggle to extract appropriate insights from large corpus of research papers available on the internet. Which means they have to resort to manual mathods impeding the speed at which innovations can be made. 
  
## **Proposed Approach**
    We aim to build a web-based research Assistant that ingests open access research papers that the user specifies and serves RAG powered  citation aware answers to their queries.
